Threshold Guidance

  • A stricter threshold (lower number) may catch more phishing but can increase false positives.
  • A more lenient threshold (higher number) may reduce quarantine volume but can miss suspicious emails.
  • The MVP uses this setting for all future scoring decisions across the platform.
